Network Diagrams of My Brilliant Friend

If the work cannot be displayed, here is a ALTERNATIVE LINK


 This Network Diagrams visualizes the social relationships between characters in the TV series My Brilliant Friend, including friendship, family, love, and work relationships. Simultaneously, I made three simplified diagrams in next three pages, which are the love relationship diagram of two heroines, the love relationship diagram of the love saint Nino, and the work relationship diagram of Naples, where the story mainly takes place.

 The information of this diagram are collected from mainly two sources, the series itself and the fans community. Fans community like Fandom already organized some relationships and relationship analysis of main characters in table, but minor characters wasn’t recorded. Therefore most of information and photos are collected from series itself.

 The social relationships between people are not single point to single point, but interlaced like a complex diagram. My brilliant Friend contains too many characters and plots, audiences may forget the relationship or the minor characters easily, it affects people's understanding of the plot(Ma and Yang, 2019). And charts as a simplifying tool for complex data can enhance people's memory (Mulrow, 2012).

 In this diagram, nodes represent characters, and depending on the size of the nodes, its easy to determine the importance of the character in the story. And names under the nodes are characters’ first names, from the color of the name, the characters can be divided into different families at a glance. Besides, links represent relationships. The weighted links often represents the strength of the character's connection, and in this table, the strength of the connection is based not only on the emotional strength of the character, but also by the amount of space the presentation of the relationship takes up in the TV series. You can also see there are arrows on the links, some are double-headed and others just single headed. The one-way arrow in this diagram represents a one-way emotional connection, which can be unrequited love or one-sided hatred. According to the above, network diagram can clearly show many-to-many relationships in one time, and help audience understand and recall the characters and plots quickly.
